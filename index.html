"Welcome to LargeBuck$ Coffee, where we take our coffee seriously but not ourselves. We're a cozy little corner of the internet — and your eighbourhood — dedicated to one simple belief: life is too short for bad coffee. Whether you're working, daydreaming, or pretending to work while actually daydreaming, we've got the perfect cup to keep you company. Pull up a chair, stay a while, and let us do the heavy lifting — one delicious brew at a time."

"Every day at LargeBuck$ is a little different, just like your mood on a Monday versus your mood on a Friday. That's why we rotate our daily specials — because variety is the spice of life, and also because our baristas get bored easily. Each day brings a new coffee, a new colour and a new reason to get out of bed in the morning. Check back every day to see what we're brewing, or just click the days above if you're the type who likes to plan ahead. We respect that."

"At LargeBuck$ we source our beans from the finest corners of the world, which is a fancy way of saying we are very picky about our coffee and completely unapologetic about it. Every cup is crafted with care, precision, and just the right amount of love — plus whatever the secret ingredient is that makes it taste so good. We'd tell you what it is, but then we'd have to make you sign a very official looking piece of paper. What we can tell you is that once you try a LargeBuck$ coffee, every other coffee will feel like a lukewarm disappointment. You've been warned."
